About Me

A Journey Towards Truth

I am Mohammed Touaha Akbar. A Genetic Engineer and Biotechnologist by academic identity. Interested in science and research. More interested in teaching. So I have taken teaching as a profession.

A devoted Muslim in moral and ideological life. Having left atheism, by the infinite mercy and grace of Allah Subhanahu wa Ta'ala, I was able to recognize the light of Islam and come to this path in 2011.

Now I am learning. I know I have to cross a long way. Not as an unfortunate person who is too late to gather the provisions for the eternal life, I want to live with the determination of building golden days. Whatever pearls I can gather on this momentary beach are the words in my profit account.

I have planted a flag here to write and speak about a pearl or two, about insights on the walking path. I will not be here one day. Some of the pearls I found might remain touching generation after generation. Maybe it will be someone's torch of light. And that fire will spread everywhere.
